About Unknown Systems

Community and citizen science, built right.

Unknown Systems is a UK-based software company focused on one thing: building platforms that let communities come together to explore, document, and understand complex questions that mainstream tools handle poorly — or not at all.

We believe structure creates signal. Most community platforms reward noise over knowledge, virality over credibility. Our systems are designed to invert that — giving contributors the tools to document experiences properly, evaluate evidence rigorously, and build archives that actually grow in value over time.

We operate with a clear ethical position: no advertising, no data selling. The people who use our platforms own their experience of them.
